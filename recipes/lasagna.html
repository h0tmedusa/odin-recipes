Lasagna

This is one of the world's finest lasagna. Lasagna is a pasta dish which is very simple to make yet is certainly considered one of the best pastas out there. This Italian dish is a global meal loved by millions across the planet.

Making lasagna can be time consuming sometimes, but the results are well worth the wait. Altough the recipe is incredibly customisable, here is a recipe given by John. Some meat, usually beef or sausage does the job. An onion and two cloves of garlic cooked with the meat adds a whole lot of flavor. To the mix, adding a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato cans and 2 cans of tomato paste can really bring that tangyness. As for sugar you will need two tablespoons of white sugar to add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavour of the sauce. Some seasoning with fresh parslet, dreid basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper.

Finally to this mix add the Lasagna noodles. You can use both canned noodles or homemade lasagna. Some parmesan cheese, some mozzarella and ricotta cheese make the lasgana extra decadent. An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna. That's it! That is all you need to get started to treat yourself with a wonderful plate of lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

Here is the above mentioned content in a list for when you go out shopping :

  • Meat: Beef or sweet Italian sausage
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic
  • Products: A can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce and tomato paste each.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons
  • Spices and seasoning: fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Canned or homemade.
  • Cheese: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg: An egg

How to make Lasgana Step-by-Step

  1. Make the meat sauce as mentioned above.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to instructions given below.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How to Layer Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the steps and top the remainig with Parmesan

Your dish is now ready to served!
